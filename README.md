# Macroeconomic Drivers of Soda Consumption in CCI Markets (2015–2024)

This project explores the relationship between key macroeconomic indicators GDP per capita, GDP PPP, and urbanization rate and non-alcoholic beverage consumption across nine Coca Cola İçecek (CCI) markets between 2015 and 2024.

The analysis includes exploratory data analysis, correlation tests, OLS regression, and time series visualization. Results suggest that purchasing power (GDP PPP) is the strongest predictor of soda consumption, while urbanization plays a secondary but still meaningful role.

**Note:** This study is based on a relatively small dataset covering 9 countries and 99 observations. Findings should be treated as exploratory rather than conclusive, and results may not generalize beyond the markets included.
